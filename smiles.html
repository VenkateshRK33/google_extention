About the Website

A webpage about emojis that express our feelings typically covers a wide range of topics, including the history, evolution, cultural significance, and practical usage of these small digital icons. The origin of emojis can be traced back to emoticons, simple text-based symbols like :-) or :-(, which were used to convey emotions in digital communication. The first set of emojis was created in Japan in the late 1990s by Shigetaka Kurita, consisting of 176 icons designed for a mobile internet platform. Over time, emojis have evolved into a global language, featuring thousands of symbols that represent a broad spectrum of emotions, objects, activities, and concepts.
